# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Nexus aims to be a respectful, thoughtful, and inclusive engineering community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and evidence-oriented.
- Respect differing perspectives and experience levels.
- Focus critique on ideas, architecture, and implementation decisions rather than people.
- Help maintain a collaborative environment for humans and AI-assisted contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or hostile behavior
- Deliberate disruption of community discussions or contribution processes
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission

## Reporting

Please report conduct concerns through the security or maintainer contact path described in `SECURITY.md` until a dedicated community channel is established.
