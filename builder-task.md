## `NEXUS-REV-2026-07-16-016` Observation 1 — `GovernanceStateProjectionService` replay-on-read is redundant with live subscription

Category 6, Observation, Informational (Sprint 63; carried forward). `getGovernanceStateProjection` maintains a live `EventBus` subscription and also unconditionally replays the Mission's entire event history on every retrieval call, re-applying already-consumed events. Harmless — dedupes by `eventId`, verified idempotent — but redundant for a continuously-subscribed Mission, and the per-event `includes()` dedup check is `O(n)`, making full replay-and-reapply `O(n²)` in the Mission's total event count. Immaterial at current scale. No Builder Task. Sprint 64's `GovernanceGatedMissionCompletionCoordinator` now relies on this replay-on-read behavior for its own correctness (verified by the Reviewer to be race-free); any future Sprint reconsidering this behavior must re-verify that reliance. A future Sprint may reconsider whether replay-on-read is needed once subscription-only freshness is proven sufficient, or use a `Set` for `consumedEventIds` lookups if event counts grow.
