# Research Corpus

## Purpose
Provide a home for exploratory research that may inform future decisions or specifications.

## Responsibilities
- Capture external findings
- Compare approaches
- Preserve sources for later evaluation

## Scope
Research is informative but not authoritative until validated through decisions or specifications.

## Future Evolution
This area should eventually distinguish raw research, synthesized findings, and adopted conclusions.

## Relationship to the Engineering Corpus
Research can strengthen Shared Reality when it is curated, cited, and connected to project decisions.
