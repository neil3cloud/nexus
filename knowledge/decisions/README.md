# Decision Corpus

## Purpose
Record durable architectural and engineering decisions.

## Responsibilities
- Maintain ADRs
- Preserve decision history and rationale
- Support traceability from implementation back to intent

## Scope
Focused on decisions with lasting impact rather than transient discussions.

## Future Evolution
The decision corpus should expand as Nexus adopts new capabilities, hosts, storage models, and execution strategies.

## Relationship to the Engineering Corpus
Decisions convert architectural reasoning into explicit Shared Reality that future contributors can reference.
