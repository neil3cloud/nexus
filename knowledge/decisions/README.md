# Decisions

## Purpose

Record durable architectural and engineering decisions.

## Responsibilities

- Maintain ADRs
- Preserve decision history and rationale
- Support traceability from implementation back to intent

## Scope

Focused on decisions with lasting impact rather than transient discussions.

## Future Evolution

This area should expand as Nexus adopts new workflow slices, hosts, adapters, and execution strategies.

## Relationship to Shared Reality

Decisions convert architectural reasoning into explicit evidence that future contributors can reference.
