## Purpose

## Status

## Definition

Shared Reality is the continuously evolving, evidence-backed understanding of a software project that Nexus maintains for humans and AI systems.

## Sources of Reality

Shared Reality is assembled from the Engineering Corpus, including code, architecture, ADRs, requirements, tests, issue history, runtime observations, and validated reasoning.

## Architectural Consequences

- Capabilities must begin with evidence assembly.
- Recommendations must be explainable.
- Project knowledge must outlive individual sessions or providers.
