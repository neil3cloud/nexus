# Roadmap Corpus

## Purpose
Describe intended platform evolution without locking the architecture into premature implementation choices.

## Responsibilities
- Track strategic themes
- Connect near-term steps to long-term direction
- Make evolution visible to contributors

## Scope
Roadmap artifacts should reflect architectural intent, not speculative promises.

## Future Evolution
This area can later evolve into milestone planning, adoption sequencing, and capability maturity maps.

## Relationship to the Engineering Corpus
The roadmap should show how Shared Reality and the Engineering Corpus will become stronger over time.
