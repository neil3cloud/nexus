# Engineering Lifecycle

## Purpose

Describe the intended flow of work in Nexus-enabled engineering.

## Scope

Covers understand, plan, act, validate, and enrich phases.

## Intended Audience

Workflow designers and architects.

## Status

Draft

## Related Documents

- context-assembly.md
- knowledge-evolution.md

## Lifecycle Shape

1. Assemble evidence
2. Form mission understanding
3. Select execution strategy and assign roles
4. Execute through adapters and providers
5. Review and validate outcomes
6. Capture reusable engineering knowledge

## Expected Outcome

Each completed activity should increase future understanding and reduce repeated rediscovery.
