# Engineering Lifecycle

## Purpose
Describe the intended flow of work in Nexus-enabled engineering.

## Scope
Covers understand, plan, act, validate, and enrich phases.

## Intended Audience
Workflow designers and platform architects.

## Status
Draft

## Related Documents
- context-assembly.md
- knowledge-evolution.md

## Lifecycle Shape

1. Assemble evidence
2. Form task understanding
3. Plan through capabilities
4. Execute through adapters and providers
5. Validate outcomes
6. Enrich the Engineering Corpus

## Expected Outcome

Each completed activity should increase future understanding and reduce repeated rediscovery.
