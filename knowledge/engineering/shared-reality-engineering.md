# Shared Reality Engineering

## Purpose
Connect Shared Reality to practical engineering behavior.

## Scope
Defines how teams and AI systems should work from shared evidence.

## Intended Audience
Contributors designing workflows and engineering policy.

## Status
Draft

## Related Documents
- ../architecture/shared-reality.md
- evidence-based-engineering.md

## Working Model

Engineering work should begin by grounding itself in corpus evidence and should end by improving that evidence for the next task.

## Anti-Pattern

Prompting without reference to corpus state creates local answers but weakens global project understanding.
