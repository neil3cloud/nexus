# Evidence-Based Engineering

## Purpose
Define the expectation that understanding must precede generation or action.

## Scope
Covers evidence collection, evaluation, and traceability expectations.

## Intended Audience
Capability designers, maintainers, and reviewers.

## Status
Foundational

## Related Documents
- context-assembly.md
- ../evidence/README.md

## Principle

Evidence should be assembled intentionally from relevant corpus artifacts before making recommendations, generating code, or planning execution.

## Explainability

Outputs should be traceable to the evidence that influenced them.
