# Context Assembly

## Purpose

Describe how relevant Shared Reality should be assembled for a task.

## Scope

Covers contextual narrowing, evidence selection, and task framing.

## Intended Audience

Engineers building context, planning, retrieval, or orchestration flows.

## Status

Draft

## Related Documents

- evidence-based-engineering.md

## Concept

Context assembly is the process of transforming repository knowledge and project signals into task-relevant evidence while preserving traceability and confidence.

## Future Direction

Future specifications should define ranking, freshness, validation, and contradiction handling without coupling those concerns to a specific retrieval technology.
