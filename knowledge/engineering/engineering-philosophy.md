# Engineering Philosophy

## Purpose
Explain the philosophy of cumulative, explainable engineering in Nexus.

## Scope
Describes how engineering knowledge is treated as a long-lived asset.

## Intended Audience
Maintainers, contributors, and AI platform designers.

## Status
Foundational

## Related Documents
- shared-reality-engineering.md
- knowledge-evolution.md

## Position

Software engineering creates knowledge, not only code. Nexus treats that knowledge as an asset that should improve over the lifetime of a project.

## Implication

Every meaningful action should leave the project easier to understand, verify, and extend.
