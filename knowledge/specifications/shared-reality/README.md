# Shared Reality Specifications

## Purpose

Provide a place for normative definitions of repository knowledge structure, evidence quality, and reality assembly.

## Responsibilities

- Define Shared Reality concepts
- Clarify evidence requirements
- Support consistency across workflow services and roles

## Scope

Focused on conceptual models, not storage engine selection.

## Future Evolution

Future specifications may address validation, confidence, freshness, and contradiction handling.

## Relationship to Shared Reality

These specifications directly govern how Shared Reality is assembled and trusted.
