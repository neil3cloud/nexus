# Workflow Specifications

## Purpose
Describe how higher-level workflows coordinate capabilities without redefining them.

## Responsibilities
- Capture workflow stages and transitions
- Define evidence checkpoints
- Preserve explainability across execution paths

## Scope
Workflows should compose capabilities rather than bypass architectural boundaries.

## Future Evolution
Future workflow specifications may cover planning loops, review flows, and corpus enrichment steps.

## Relationship to the Engineering Corpus
Well-defined workflows ensure Shared Reality remains central even as automation expands.
