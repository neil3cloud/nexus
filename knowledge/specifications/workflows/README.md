# Workflow Specifications

## Purpose

Describe how higher-level workflows coordinate roles, review, and knowledge capture without redefining kernel boundaries.

## Responsibilities

- Capture workflow stages and transitions
- Define evidence checkpoints
- Preserve explainability across execution paths

## Scope

Workflows should compose kernel services and role assignments rather than bypass architectural boundaries.

## Future Evolution

Future workflow specifications may cover planning loops, review flows, approval gates, and knowledge capture steps.

## Relationship to Shared Reality

Well-defined workflows ensure Shared Reality remains central even as automation expands.
