# Nexus Glossary

## Shared Reality

The continuously evolving, evidence-backed understanding of a software project used by humans and AI systems.

## Mission

An explicit engineering objective derived from a developer request.

## Execution Strategy

The kernel decision that determines which engineering roles participate in a mission and in what order.

## Role

A stable engineering responsibility such as Builder or Reviewer.

## Host

A user-facing surface such as VS Code, CLI, web, or another IDE.

## Adapter

A boundary component that integrates Nexus contracts with external systems.

## Provider

A concrete implementation used to execute an assigned engineering role through a defined contract.
