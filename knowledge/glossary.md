# Nexus Glossary

## Shared Reality
The continuously evolving, evidence-backed understanding of a software project used by humans and AI systems.

## Engineering Corpus
The complete body of engineering knowledge representing Shared Reality.

## Capability
A stable architectural behavior exposed by the platform independent of any vendor or runtime.

## Host
A user-facing surface such as VS Code, CLI, web, or another IDE.

## Adapter
A boundary component that integrates Nexus contracts with external systems.

## Provider
A concrete implementation used to execute a capability through a defined contract.
