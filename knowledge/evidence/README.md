# Evidence Corpus

## Purpose
Reserve space for evidence artifacts that support engineering understanding and explainability.

## Responsibilities
- Capture supporting observations
- Preserve links between claims and proof
- Enable auditability of recommendations

## Scope
Focused on evidence and validation artifacts rather than broad documentation.

## Future Evolution
Future evidence may include traces, benchmark outputs, runtime observations, or validation records.

## Relationship to the Engineering Corpus
Evidence is the mechanism by which Shared Reality becomes trustworthy rather than aspirational.
